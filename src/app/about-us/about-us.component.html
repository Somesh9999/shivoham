We are one of the leading retail suppliers of all types of laptops, computers, printers and all its peripherals, since 2008. We are committed to prompt supplies with best quality and prompt services. The company was established by K. V. Rameshan Nair in 2008 with sole intention and aim to be in the market as a quality branded supplier and named the organisation as Shiva Shakthi Electrical Stampings understanding the then demand for power press metal cutting products in the machinery and transformer manufacturing industry also. Earlier the company was known as Shivam Electricals supplying all computer related products, servicing and repairing.

SHIVOHAM ENTERPRISES is a family owned business with its branches in Maharashtra at Mumbai and Pune. It also has a distribution in Kerala at Kannur.

Shiva Shakthi Electrical Stampings is a company which deals with power press machines in which E & I , CRGO and CRNO and various other types of metal sheets are cut and delivered to various companies. These E & I sheets are used in making of transformer parts largely. There are various other uses of these sheets with respect to the mass production of machineries.

During mid 2020 the company could experience the manufacturing and market requirement and increasing demand of online supplies. It was then that the planning of two different units with its own identity came into existence. By the beginning of 2021 Mr. K. V. Rameshan Nair formed a new venture with his family owned business in its synonymous name. SHIVOHAM ENTERPRISES was registered in the name of his wife as proprietor of the company to continue its computer related business.
